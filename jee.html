JEE - Joint Entrance Examination

Binomial Shortcuts

Use binomial theorem for quick expansion, like (a+b)^n. Remember special cases: (a+b)^2, (a-b)^2, (a+b)(a-b). Pascal triangle for coefficients.

Trigonometry Shortcuts

Learn unit circle values, basic trigonometric identities, and common trigonometric ratios. Memorize key angles. Apply properties to simplify expressions.Practice well.

Quadratic Shortcuts

Factorize quickly, use the quadratic formulae efficiently, identify the perfect squares, and employ Vieta's formulas for sum of roots and product of roots

Inverse Trigo Shortcuts

Memorize standard values of inverse trigonometric functions, understand their principal ranges, and apply trigonometric identities to simplify expressions .

Using Formulas Efficiently

Memorize and apply important mathematical formulas, especially those related to geometry, algebra, and arithmetic. Being familiar with the formulas is important.
